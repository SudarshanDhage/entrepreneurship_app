## The Flywheel Effect

- **Source:** Atlassian Blog (Work Life)
- **Author:** Cameron Deatsch (Chief Revenue Officer, Atlassian)
- **URL:** https://www.atlassian.com/blog/strategy/flywheel-effect-10-lessons
- **Date:** 2025
- **Featured:** Cameron Deatsch, Jim Collins (Good to Great book)
- **Key Concepts:** Flywheel effect, small wins accumulating into momentum, Jim Collins' flywheel model, three components (Attract, Engage, Delight), reducing friction, self-serve purchase flow, customers as best salespeople, products should be bought not sold, low-touch highly automated GTM, 15% revenue on GTM
- **Key Insight:** The flywheel effect occurs when small wins accumulate over time, creating momentum that keeps your business growing. Once you get a flywheel going, it takes relatively little effort to keep it spinning or make it spin faster.
