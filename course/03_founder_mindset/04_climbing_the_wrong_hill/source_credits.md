## Climbing the Wrong Hill

- **Source:** cdixon.org
- **Author:** Chris Dixon
- **URL:** https://cdixon.org/2009/09/19/climbing-the-wrong-hill
- **Date:** 2025
- **Featured:** Chris Dixon
- **Key Insight:** People systematically overvalue near-term rewards over long-term rewards, especially ambitious people. When you know you want to be on a different hill, don't waste time climbing the current one.
